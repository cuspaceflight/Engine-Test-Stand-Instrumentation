# Servo Motor Specifications

### Environmental & Physical Specifications
* **Storage Temperature Range:** -30 ~ 80 °C
* **Operating Temperature Range:** -20 ~ 60 °C
* **Weight:** 67 ± 1 g
* **Case Material:** PA + Aluminum

### Mechanical Specifications
* **Gear Type:** Steel
* **Limit Angle:** No limit
* **Bearing Type:** Ball bearings
* **Horn Gear Spline:** 25T / 5.9 mm
* **Horn Type:** Plastic, POM

### Electrical Specifications
* **Motor Type:** Core Motor
* **Operating Voltage Range:** 6.0 V - 7.4 V
* **Idle Current (at stopped):** 6 mA @ 7.4 V
* **Running Current (at no load):** 180 mA @ 7.4 V
* **Stall Current:** 3.9 A @ 7.4 V

### Performance Specifications
* **No-load Speed:** 0.192 sec / 60° @ 7.4 V
* **Peak Stall Torque:** 35.5 kg·cm @ 7.4 V
* **Rated Torque:** 11.8 kg·cm @ 7.4 V

### Control & Signal Specifications
* **Command Signal:** Pulse Width Modulation (PWM)
* **Amplifier Type:** Digital comparator
* **Pulse Width Range:** 500 ~ 2500 μsec
* **Stop Position:** 1500 μsec
* **Running Degree:** 220° Continuous Rotation
* **Dead Band Width:** ≤ 4 μsec
* **Rotating Direction:** Counterclockwise (1500 - 2000 μsec)

### Pin Definition
* **Pin 1:** Signal
* **Pin 2:** Vcc
* **Pin 3:** GND
